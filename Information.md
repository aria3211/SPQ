# what is the SQP?
* __SQP__ is a abreviated word Solve programming questions.  You can put any questions you have solved along with the answers here so that others can learn to solve their problems.This will be of great help to the junior developers as well as share the resources that have helped you solve the problem.


### Authentic sources for finding standard questions


* [codewars](https://www.codewars.com/)
* [Quera](https://quera.ir/problemset)
* [TopCoder](https://www.topcoder.com/)
* [coderbyte](https://coderbyte.com/e)
* [HackerRank](https://www.hackerrank.com/)
